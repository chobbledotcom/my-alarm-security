## A recent job in Petts Wood

We were called to a property in Petts Wood where the homeowner had their car stolen off the driveway. The thieves had used some sort of jamming device to access the car and start it - the alarm system in the house hadn't been triggered because the car was taken without any forced entry to the property. The existing alarm was over 20 years old and had never been used.

We upgraded the whole [burglar alarm system](/categories/burglar-alarms/) to a Pyronix smart system and added the [Pyronix car defender](/blog/car-defender-by-pyronix/) for their new car. The car defender wraps round the steering wheel and when it feels vibration or is tilted, it triggers the house alarm siren. We also fitted a 5 camera [CCTV system](/categories/cctv/) - two cameras at the front, one down the side and two at the rear with one covering the shed entrance. Both systems have app control so the homeowner can check everything from their phone.
