## An alarm system for a deaf customer near Greenhithe

We had a call from a lady in Dartford on the borders of Greenhithe and Ebbsfleet who wanted to upgrade her alarm system, but with one difference - the owner was deaf. The alarm siren at 100+ decibels inside and outside the property would obviously not alert her, so we fitted a Pyronix wireless sounder equipped with a super bright strobe. The strobe was set up to light up on entry and exit, lighting the way to the keypad or front door, and to flash on an activation of the house alarm. The customer also had a strobe in her bedroom which would wake her up if the alarm was triggered, much like an alarm clock or fire alarm.
