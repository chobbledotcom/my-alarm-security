How to Choose the Right CCTV System

Published: February 20, 2024

Consider Your Needs

Think about what areas you need to monitor and the level of detail required.

Indoor vs Outdoor Cameras

Choose weatherproof cameras for outdoor use and compact models for indoor monitoring.

Resolution Matters

Higher resolution cameras provide clearer images for identification purposes.

Storage Options

Consider local storage, cloud storage, or hybrid solutions based on your requirements.
