We recently received a call from a customer in [Eltham](/pages/eltham/). We had been recommended to this customer on a social media platform by several people in their area who had used - and were happy with our services. We attended the property and did a survey for a [burglar alarm system](/categories/burglar-alarms/) and an upgrade from a DIY [CCTV system](/categories/cctv/). We went through the property and discussed the customer's needs. The customer wanted to upgrade their DIY CCTV system to a more powerful and reliable system after recent break-ins in the area. The customer opted for our 4 camera Hikvision ColourVu CCTV setup along with a Pyronix burglar alarm system - both with smart app integration, allowing the customer full access to both systems from anywhere in the world. You can see our current [CCTV Package 1](/products/cctv-package-2-1199-24hr-colour-cctv/) and [CCTV Package 2](/products/cctv-package-1-999/) options, or the combined [Ultimate Package](/products/supreme-package-24hr-colour-cctv-plus-intruder-alarm-system-1749/) which includes both alarm and CCTV. 

The DIY system that they had, had been fitted a few years previously by a 'so called professional'. We removed all of the system including all of the existing cabling which had been clipped to the exterior walls which looked rather unsightly. Luckily the customer still had some paint left over from when the exterior of their property was painted. We patched up the holes left by the old cables and painted over them. Then we went about installing a professionally fitted system with concealed cables, keeping the outside of the house looking neat and tidy. You can see from the before and after pics the quality of our work - along with a much more powerful camera capable of full colour night time vision. 

This customer was left very happy with their brand new systems and no longer had unattractive cables sagging and running around their home.
